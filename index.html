About the Conference

ICEITF is a premier global platform for researchers, academicians, and practitioners to explore and exchange ideas on the latest advancements in engineering and technology.

The conference aims to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among experts in various fields of engineering and technology, with a focus on innovative applications that can shape a better future for humanity.

ICEITF will feature keynote speakers from leading institutions, paper presentations, panel discussions, and interactive sessions covering cutting-edge research and practical applications.

Call for Papers

Submit your original research to be showcased at ICEITF.

Submission Guidelines

  • Original, unpublished manuscripts not under review elsewhere.
  • Prepared in English using the official conference template.
  • Submitted through the ICEITF online submission portal only.
  • Peer-reviewed by domain experts appointed by the technical committee.
  • Limit of 8 pages per paper (including figures, tables, and references).

Publication Opportunities

All accepted papers will appear in the official ICEITF proceedings. Selected high-quality, extended versions will be invited for publication in special issues of renowned SCOPUS Indexed journals and partner book series.
